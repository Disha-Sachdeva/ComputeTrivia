Get to Know Us!



ComputeTrivia is your ultimate destination for testing your knowledge of computer science trivia! Whether you're a seasoned programmer or just curious about the world of technology, we have a wide range of trivia questions to challenge and entertain you.

How it works?

  • Explore our collection of trivia categories, including programming languages, computer history, software development, and more.
  • Choose a category and test your knowledge with our fun and engaging trivia questions.
  • Answer it out loud to your teacher or peers and flip the flashcard to check your answer.
  • Keep coming back for new trivia questions and challenges added regularly!
